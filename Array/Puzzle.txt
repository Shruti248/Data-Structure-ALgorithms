Puzzle : 
Youhave 15 rupees 
 --> 1 chocoloate cost 1 rupee 
 --> with 3 wrappers you can buy 1 chocolate
Find maximum chocolates 

 ANS : 15(with rs) + 5(with wrappers i.e 15/3 = 5) + 1(with 3 rappers of the 5 chocloates last bought and 2 are left) + 1(2 left ones + the 1 chocolate recently bought) = 15+5+1+1 = 22
